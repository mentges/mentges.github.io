About

Engineer first. Consultant second.

I'm Bruno Mentges — a Senior Data & AI Engineer based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I've spent nearly three decades building production systems, from fintech payment processors to petabyte-scale ad platforms.

Based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil (GMT−3)

At a top-10 global ad tech platform, I worked on data pipelines processing 1.5 billion records per day at petabyte scale.
